Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-28

1. What We Collect

When you use the 4CGT generation service, we collect:

  • Account information: email address, display name, and authentication credentials.
  • Generation data: prompts, generation parameters, and image hashes (for content safety compliance).
  • Usage data: IP address, timestamps, and request metadata.
  • Payment data: processed by our third-party payment provider. We do not store credit card numbers.

2. Why We Collect It

  • Service operation: to process your generation requests and manage your account.
  • Content safety: to detect and prevent prohibited content, and to comply with legal reporting obligations (including NCMEC CyberTipline reporting for detected CSAM).
  • Abuse prevention: to enforce rate limits, detect fraud, and prevent Terms of Service violations.

3. Data Retention

  • Generated images: not stored on our servers after delivery to you, unless flagged by content safety systems.
  • Flagged content: retained for 90 days as required by law, or until requested by law enforcement.
  • Prompts and metadata: retained for 90 days for safety audit purposes, then deleted.
  • Account data: retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account closure.

4. Who We Share With

  • Law enforcement: when required by law, or when content safety systems detect CSAM or other illegal content.
  • NCMEC: detected CSAM is reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children as required by US law (18 USC 2258A).
  • Payment processors: payment data is shared with our payment provider to process transactions.
  • Infrastructure providers: generation requests are processed on third-party GPU infrastructure. Prompts and images pass through these systems transiently during generation.

We do not sell your data to third parties.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. EU/EEA users have additional rights under GDPR, including data portability and the right to object to processing.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

This website uses localStorage to remember your age verification status. No tracking cookies are used. The generation service uses session cookies for authentication.

7. Children

The Service is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover that a minor has created an account, we will terminate it immediately.

8. Changes

We may update this policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated via the Service.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/teenu/4cgt/issues.
